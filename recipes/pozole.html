Pozole Recipe

Red pozole is the most comun recipe from Veracruz, is an aromatic broth that mix oregano, bay leaf and thyme with guajillo chiles.

The flavor of this recipe is not spicy, if you lice spacy meals you can serve with ground pikin chiles.

Prep time: Total time: Servings:
30 min 3.3 h 20-2420

INGREDIENTS

  • 7 onces dried guajillo chiles
  • 3 kg meet (pork rib or solid meat)
  • 3 bags precooked corn
  • 2 whole garlic
  • 1 bunch fresh or 3 tablespoons dry oregano
  • 1 bunch fresh or 2 tablespoons dry twig
  • 1 bunch fresh or 2 tablespoons dry thyme
  • 1 white onion, 1/2 chopped
  • 1 lettuce
  • 1 bunch red radishes, chopped
  • 4 limes, quartered
  • Tostadas (fried tortillas)
  • Ground Pikin chiles
  • Salt to season

INSTRUCTIONS

Step 1

Remove and discard the stems, seeds, and large veins from chiles. Heat the chilles for both sides over a heat iron pan, for acuple minute, do not ket them burn. Add the chiles into the pot hot water, cover and let them for 15 to 20 min. Blend chilles whit 4 cloves garlic, 1/4 onion, reserve.

Step 2

Add pork, previously washed, and spices (Put the spices into a small piece of cloth and tie it), and a whole garlic to large pot of boiling water for 2.5 h.

Step 3

Add the precooked corn into a large pot of boiling water with a whole garli, let cook for about 15 min, then add to the meat pot right at the end of browning th meat (until the pork is completely tender2.6 h)

Step 4

Add the blend chilles passing the sauce through a strainer let boil with meat for the las 20 minutes.

Step 5

When getting ready to serve the pozole, you can prep the garnishes. Choop the onion, red radishes and lettuce (previously desinfected) and serve into separate bowls. Also you can add pikin chilles and dry oregano to increase the spicy and aromatic flavors of the pozole. Serve the pozole broth into bowls with tostadas (fried tortillas
