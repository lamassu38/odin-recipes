Mole with Chicken Recipe

This type of mole mix the strong flavor of dried ancho chiles, with sweet chocolate, plantain, peanuts, cookies and more. Learn how to make this delicius dish.

Mole is my favorite Mexican dish. I'm from Veracruz and this is my family recipe. I consider this recipe to be sweeter comparered to others, because of the chocolate. With leftover mole sauce you can prepare enmoladas (mole enchiladas), or put some on eggs on a tortilla. If you enjoy home cooked meals you should try this recipe. Enjoy!

Prep time: Total time: Servings:
40 min 2 h 12

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 chicken
  • 1 onion- chopped
  • 1/3 cup almons
  • 1/3 cup peanuts
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 8 cloves garlic
  • 500 gr dried ancho chiles
  • 70 gr cookies - an option Maria
  • 2 bars of chocolate - an option Abuelita
  • 1 tablespon of toasted sesame seeds
  • 4 tortillas
  • 7 tomatoes
  • salt to taste

INSTRUCTIONS

Step 1

Clean and sear the chicken directly over the fire for a few seconds and ut it into a saucepan. Filled with wather until the midle, add a head of garlic and salt to taste and then let it simmer for 20 minutes. Remove an reserve the chicken.

Step 2

Toast the peanut, almonds and cinnamon and add to the chicken soup. Fry the next ingridients: tortilla, cookies, onion, garlic, tomatoes, plantain and ancho chiles. Add all ingredients and blend and strain then. Heat the sauce over low heat during 20-30 minutes, do not stop moving.

Step 3

Served with chicken and warm with rice and tortillas and sprinled with toasted sesame seeds.
